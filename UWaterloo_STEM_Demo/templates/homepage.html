PROMOTING STUDENT SUCCESS WITH DATA DRIVEN GUIDANCE

The Challenge

There is an underrepresentation of Women in STEM undergraduate degrees, graduate degrees, and careers.

Our Method

Analyze University of Waterloo student data using machine learning techniques to identify factors that lead to women student success and identify strategies to increase enrolment, academic success, and careers growth in STEM.

Our Findings

Students were grouped into 6 main clusters with distinct attributes, preferences, and motivations. This clusters enable the University of Waterloo to begin developing targeted programs to support and encourage more female students to pursue opportunities in STEM.
